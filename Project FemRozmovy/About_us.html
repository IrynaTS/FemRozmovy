About us

The project was founded in 2021 for popularizing the ideas of feminism among women through the discussion of movie, TV-series, reading books and discussions. This is the platform where women openly talk in a safe environment about topics that concern them. What we do: we read books, watch movies and series and meet to discuss them once a month online in ZOOM.
